Who are the Arctic Monkeys ?

The Arctic Monkeys are an English indie rock band that started in Sheffield in 2002, they have 7 albums and at least 24 singles and they started to get popular in the US for songs like “Why’d you only call me when you're high”,”505” and “Do I wanna know” but those aren't the only good songs they have.
